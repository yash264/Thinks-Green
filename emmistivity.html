To determine the emissivity of a gray surface at different temperatures .

According to Stefan - Boltzmann law, the radiation energy emitted by a black body is proportional to the fourth power of the absolute temperature.
Q = σAT4 where σ = Stefan Boltzman Constant= 5.6697 x 10-8 W/m2 K4

Emissivity of a surface is a measure of how it emits radiant energy in comparison with a black body at the same temperature, or emissivity of a surface is a ratio of emissive power of the surface to the emissive power of a perfect black body at the same temperature.
Є=E/Eb
Є = Emissivity E = Emissive power of the gray body Eb= Emissive power of the perfect black body

To determine the convective heat transfer co-efficent for heated vertical cylinder losing heat to the ambient by free or natural convection.

Corvection heat transfer occurs by the movement of fluid particles. If the motion of fluid partides occurs by the variation of density of the fluid due to temperature difference, then the heat transfer process Is called free or natural convection. The rate of heat transfer in convection is glven by the Newton's Law of cooling i.e. q= h*A*T where h = Convective or film heat transfer co efficient, h depends upon the fluid properties, type of flow and geometry of the surface.
